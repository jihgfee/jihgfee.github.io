Short Biography

I am a postdoc at Aarhus University in Denmark. I come from an applied background, and so my interests for theoretical computer science are rooted in bridging the gap between industry and academia. My main contribution thus far, the Actris logical framework, is then, in part, a venture about defining intuitive yet expressive specifications and proofs for complicated message-passing programs, parsable by those who are less theoretically interested.
